## Test Flow Stages

Each candidate will progress through:
1. ✅ **Registration** - Sign up with email
2. ✅ **Profile Completion** - Fill in all details
3. ⬜ **Resume Upload/Build** - AI resume builder
4. ⬜ **Job Browse** - View available jobs
5. ⬜ **Job Application** - Apply to matched jobs
6. ⬜ **Application Review** - Recruiter reviews
7. ⬜ **Interview Scheduled** - Recruiter books interview
8. ⬜ **Interview Completed** - Notes/feedback
9. ⬜ **Offer Extended** - Salary, start date
10. ⬜ **Offer Accepted** - Candidate accepts
11. ⬜ **Onboarding** - Pre-employment tasks
12. ⬜ **Placement Active** - Working with client
